## Description

> The image shows a modern eyewear shop — but this is no ordinary building.
> It is the starting point of one of the bloodiest and most significant events
> of the 20th century. The event that happened here divided human history into
> "before" and "after."
>
> Your task: find the historical weapon used there — the manufacturer, model,
> and serial number.

## Solution

The challenge references the **assassination of Archduke Franz Ferdinand**
in Sarajevo (1914), which triggered World War I.

**Gavrilo Princip's weapon:**
- Manufacturer: **FN** (Fabrique Nationale d'Armes de Guerre)
- Model: **FN Model 1910**
- Serial number: **19074**

*Research path:*
1. Identify the building in the image (Sarajevo, Bosnia and Herzegovina)
2. Research the historical event at that location
3. Find documented records of the weapon used
4. Check museum catalogs and historical archives for the serial number
